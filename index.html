Top three activities to do in Aden

Swiming and Diving

Aden is a floating city in the sea with a picturesque nature that combines sea water, golden sandy beaches, and extended mountain topography.

Sirah Castle

It is a military site in the city of Aden and is used by the Yemeni army now. It was built in the eleventh century AD. It is a volcanic island located near the port of Aden. The castle is open all day, 24 hours a day.

Aden cisterns

Known as the Taweelah Cisterns, it was built in the first millennium before the birth of Christ. It is a system of water drains to take advantage of rain water.
